Types of Degree

Undergraduate study

The Faculty of Engineering offers the four year Bachelor of Engineering with Honours BE(Hons) degree - an undergraduate honours degree, that leads to a career as a professional engineer or to postgraduate study in engineering.

Postgraduate study

The Faculty offers postgraduate study in engineering following on from the undergraduate Bachelor of Engineering degree.

The following qualifications are offered:

  • Master of Computer Science
  • Master of Engineering
  • Master of Engineering Practice
  • Master of Software Development PhD
