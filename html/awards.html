Postgraduate scholarships

Victoria University offers a range of scholarships for postgraduate study.
Scholarships of particular interest to students in the School of Engineering and Computer Science are listed below.

Further information on all scholarships can be found on the Scholarships web page.

Victoria PhD and Doctoral Scholarships

Application for these scholarships is made as part of the application for admission to the PhD programme.
The same closing dates of 1 March, 1 July and 1 November each year apply.

Details and application form.

Victoria Masters Scholarships

These scholarships are for students enrolling in Masters Part 2, by thesis.
Applications close on 1 November. Value $15,000 per year + domestic fees for 1 year.

Details and application form.
